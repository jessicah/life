Castelli

Yay! My new Castelli top arrived! I don’t know how long it’s been sitting in the mail cubicle over at reception, but I have it now! Just a shame that it’s raining…

I was really worried about the size; ordering online for clothing is difficult enough as it is. But throw in the fact it’s cycling clothes, and the difficulties explode. The sizing for cycling clothes generally don’t follow normal clothing sizes very well. For example, this top is a medium; I’m typically a size small.

So I rushed to the bathroom to try in on, and it seems to fit well (breathes sigh of relief)! Must get some pictures once the weather improves… and get my sexy on with this outfit out cycling…
