Cycling Kit

So my current cycling kit is getting a little more than worn out. Therefore, it must be time for new kit. But does one waste what's already a lot of money for some off-the-shelf kit, or look into custom cycling kit options? Usually, the minimum order stuff can push it out of the reach of a single consumer, so it's going to take a bit of searching around to find custom kit that has low order minimums without individual items being ridiculously expensive.

Potential Custom Kits

  • Kalas
  • BioRacer
  • Cuore
  • NoPinz
  • Champion Systems

I already have a basic idea of what I'd like for my custom kit. It definitely needs a lot of work, but it's a beginning. Beginnings are always the hardest part, iterating from a beginning gets easier, and I'm liking where I'm already at.
